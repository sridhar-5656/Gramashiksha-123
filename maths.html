Basic Math Operations

  • Addition (+: యోజన): 5 + 3 = 8
  • Subtraction (−: వ్యత్యయం): 9 − 4 = 5
  • Multiplication (×: గుణకం): 6 × 3 = 18
  • Division (÷: భాగహారము): 12 ÷ 4 = 3
