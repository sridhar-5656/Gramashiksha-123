Quick Info

• Telugu has independent vowel letters (అచ్చులు) and consonants (హల్లులు). A consonant combines with a vowel sign (మాత్ర) to form a syllable — this combination is called గుణింతం (Gunintam). The basic inherent vowel is అ (a).

• Use the Gunintālu panel to pick a consonant (e.g., క) and see all its forms (కా, కి, కీ, కు …).
